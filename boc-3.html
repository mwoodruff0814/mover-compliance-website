What is a BOC-3?

A BOC-3 form (Designation of Agents for Service of Process) designates legal process agents in every state where your company operates. This is required by FMCSA for all interstate motor carriers.

If legal papers need to be served to your company, the process agent receives them on your behalf. Without a valid BOC-3 on file, your operating authority cannot be activated.

We partner with established blanket agents to provide coverage in all 50 states plus Washington D.C.

When You Need a BOC-3

  • New operating authority application
  • Transferring from another blanket agent
  • Reinstating revoked authority
  • Annual renewal to maintain coverage

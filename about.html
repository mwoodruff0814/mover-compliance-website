Our Story

Interstate Compliance Solutions was founded by moving industry veterans who experienced firsthand the challenges of maintaining FMCSA compliance while running a busy moving operation.

We saw too many good movers struggle with compliance requirements - not because they didn't care, but because they were too busy actually moving people to navigate the complex web of federal regulations. Late-night paperwork sessions, confusing requirements, and the constant fear of fines became the norm.

So we built the solution we wished we had: a simple, affordable, one-stop shop for all FMCSA compliance needs. No more hunting for separate arbitration programs, tariff publishers, and BOC-3 agents. No more confusion about what's required and when.
